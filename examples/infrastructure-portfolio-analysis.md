**Evidence:**
- Analyst targets rising (265+ consensus)
- $15B backlog indicates strong forward demand
- No AI capability can replace physical thermal management

### Three-Lever Assessment

| Lever | Status | Trend |
|-------|--------|-------|
| P/E | ~45x | Stable (growth premium) |
| EV/Revenue | ~4x | Expanding with AI capex |
| WACC | Standard | No stress indicators |

### Tactical vs Structural

Recent 7% dip was **TACTICAL** (profit-taking + hyperscaler cooling headline). Fundamentals unchanged. Buying opportunity.

### Jevons Paradox: **STRONGLY FAVORABLE** ✅

AI dramatically increases compute density → massively increases cooling requirements. More efficient AI doesn't reduce cooling needs — it concentrates them.

- Previous: 5-10kW per rack
- AI era: 40-100kW per rack
- Jevons outcome: Total cooling demand explodes

### Build-vs-Buy Vulnerability: **LOW** ✅

Customers cannot DIY enterprise thermal management:
- Requires physical engineering expertise
- Regulatory and safety certifications
- Integration with existing infrastructure
- Support and maintenance requirements

No AI agent can "vibe code" a cooling system.

### Survival Fitness: **STRONG** ✅

- FCF: Positive and growing
- SBC: Disciplined
- Debt: Manageable ($2.1B bond raise at favorable terms)
- Strategic positioning: Clear AI infrastructure thesis

### Overall Assessment

**CORE HOLDING** — Textbook picks-and-shovels play. Benefits from AI buildout regardless of who wins. Low disruption risk, strong Jevons tailwind.
